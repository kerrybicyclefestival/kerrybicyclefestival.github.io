Annual Picnic Cycle to Fenit

Sunday 22 September 2:00 pm
  • Tralee Town Square
  • Car Free Day, Fenit, Tralee
With something of the momentous occasion about it, this year we’ll cycle to Fenit on the first phase of the Tralee-Fenit Greenway. A long-time coming, we can celebrate in Fenit with a cuppa and a swim if you fancy.
