Frame

This year the Kerry Bicycle Festival includes a visual art element, graciously hosted in Gallery Siamsa Tire.
Frame features photography, sculpture, sound installations, and more.
Frame will take place in the gallery Siamsa Tire, the exhibition launch will take place at 6pm on Saturday 9th of May, and will run until the 16th of May, but we'd love to see you at the opening!
