Bicycle Picnic

Whoever feels like a slightly more challenging (though not too challenging) cycle is
invited to come along on the annual picnic cycle out to Fenit.

This year our cycle coincides with the Fenit Regatta. The Beach Cafe will have their infamous brownies to the ready!

Meeting Point: Tralee Town Square
Date: Sunday 16th Sept.
Time: 2 – 5pm
